# Humo AI Growth KPI Interpretation Guide

## 1) DAU / MAU (Stickiness)
- **Formula:** `DAU / MAU * 100`
- **How to read:**
  - 20–30%: normal for utility/education products.
  - 30–50%: strong habitual usage.
  - `<20%`: users are not returning often; improve retention loops.
- **Action levers:** daily streaks, reminders, short lesson nudges.

## 2) Conversion Rate
- **Formula:** `Active paid users / MAU * 100`
- **How to read:**
  - `<3%`: weak monetization funnel.
  - 3–8%: healthy early-stage.
  - `>8%`: strong value perception.
- **Action levers:** paywall timing, trial-to-paid onboarding, pricing tests.

## 3) Feature Usage Stats
- **Metrics:** total events, unique users, engagement rate by feature.
- **How to read:**
  - high events + low unique users = heavy usage by a small segment.
  - high unique users + low events = broad adoption but shallow usage.
- **Action levers:** promote underused sticky features in home feed.

## 4) Revenue Analytics
- **MRR:** recurring subscription revenue from active plans.
- **ARPU:** `MRR / MAU`.
- **How to read:**
  - MRR up + ARPU flat: growth driven mostly by user volume.
  - MRR up + ARPU up: pricing/packaging and expansion are working.

## 5) Churn Tracking
- **Formula:** `Canceled subscriptions in 30d / subscriptions active 30d ago * 100`
- **How to read:**
  - `<5%`: good retention.
  - 5–10%: monitor onboarding and product-fit segments.
  - `>10%`: urgent retention issue.
- **Action levers:** win-back offers, churn reason collection, early warning triggers.

## 6) Abuse Flags
- **Metrics:** open abuse flags and `open flags / MAU * 100`.
- **How to read:**
  - rising abuse rate can distort growth and monetization metrics.
- **Action levers:** automated risk scoring, stricter payment checks, moderation SLA.
